Roasted Red Potatoes

Description

Roasted red potatoes are a simple yet delicious dish that brings out the natural sweetness and flavor of the potatoes. The red potatoes, with their thin skins, hold up well when roasted, becoming perfectly crispy on the outside while staying soft and tender on the inside. When seasoned with a blend of herbs like rosemary, thyme, or garlic, the potatoes take on a savory depth that enhances their earthy flavor. The roasting process allows the natural sugars in the potatoes to caramelize, giving them a golden-brown, crispy exterior that contrasts beautifully with the smooth and creamy texture inside.

This dish is incredibly versatile, as it can be customized with different seasonings and ingredients. The key to perfect roasted red potatoes is ensuring they cook evenly, with a balance of crispiness and tenderness. The rich flavor of olive oil, along with the seasoning, helps create that perfect crispy coating, while the inside of t he potato remains soft and fluffy. Whether served as a side dish for a family dinner or a complement to grilled meats, roasted red potatoes are a comforting and flavorful addition to any meal.

Ingredientss

  • Red potatoes. 2 to 3 pounds, cut into quarters or bite-sized chunks
  • Olive oil. 2 to 3 tablespoons
  • Garlic. 2 to 3 cloves, minced (or 1 tsp garlic powder)
  • Salt. 1 to 1½ teaspoons
  • Black pepper. ½ teaspoon

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Wash and cut red potatoes into quarters or bite-sized pieces.
  3. Place potatoes in a large mixing bowl.
  4. Drizzle with olive oil (2 to 3 tablespoons).
  5. Add minced garlic, salt, pepper, and any optional herbs or spices.
  6. Toss well to coat all the potatoes evenly.
  7. Spread potatoes in a single layer on a baking sheet (use parchment paper or foil for easier cleanup).
  8. Roast in the preheated oven for 35 to 45 minutes, flipping halfway through for even browning.
  9. Check for doneness: potatoes should be golden and crispy outside, tender inside.
  10. Optionally, sprinkle with fresh parsley or Parmesan cheese right after baking.
  11. Serve hot and enjoy!
